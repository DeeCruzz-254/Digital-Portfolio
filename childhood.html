Formative Years

The Foundation of My Technical Journey

Formative Logic

My journey began with an analytical mindset, often found dissecting family financial patterns and the mechanics of early home computers. These formative moments shaped a personality built on precision, risk assessment, and technical curiosity.

Early Influences

Growing up in Nairobi, I was exposed to the digital revolution at an early age. While my peers played games, I was curious about how systems worked. I took apart old computers, rewired circuits, and questioned every technological limitation I encountered.

Building Block Moments

First Programs

My first lines of code were written in Python at age 12. Simple scripts that automated mundane tasks sparked a passion that hasn't faded since.

Math & Logic

Mathematics became my playground. Algorithms, cryptography, and computational theory fascinated me more than any video game ever could.

Entrepreneurial Spirit

By age 16, I had launched my first tech project - a simple web platform for local businesses. It taught me about real-world constraints and the importance of user-centric design.

The Journey Continues

These childhood experiences weren't just hobbies—they were the substrate upon which my professional identity is built. Every system I architect, every line of code I write, and every security vulnerability I hunt stems from that early curiosity and desire to understand how things work.
